Scribbletune

Create music with JavaScript & Node.js!

Patterns

Scribbletune patterns are made up of only 3 characters. the letter x, - (hyphen) and _(underscore).

  • x: Denotes note on
  • -: Denotes note off
  • _: It is placed right after an x to denote a sustain. Multiple _ denote longer sustain
